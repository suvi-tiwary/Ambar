# 🌤️ Ambar — AI-Powered Climate Digital Twin of India

> **Ambar is a living intelligence system for India's climate — transforming satellite data, weather models, and AI into real-time predictions for weather, agriculture, and disaster resilience.**

---

## 🚀 Overview

Ambar is an advanced **AI + Climate Intelligence platform** that builds a **Digital Twin of India’s environment**. It integrates:

- 🛰️ Satellite imagery & remote sensing data  
- 🌦️ Real-time weather APIs  
- 🌾 Crop & agriculture intelligence  
- 🌊 Disaster prediction systems (floods, heatwaves, droughts)  
- 🤖 AI/ML models for forecasting & pattern detection  

The goal is to help **farmers, researchers, policymakers, and citizens** make smarter, faster, and data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

India faces increasing challenges due to:

- Unpredictable weather patterns  
- Crop failures and agricultural risks  
- Delayed disaster response  
- Fragmented environmental data systems  

There is no unified platform that converts raw climate data into **actionable intelligence in real time**.

---

## 💡 Solution — Ambar

Ambar solves this by creating a **single AI-powered climate brain for India**.

It:
- Predicts weather with AI-enhanced models  
- Monitors crop health using satellite insights  
- Detects early signs of floods, droughts, and heatwaves  
- Visualizes climate changes in real-time  
- Provides region-wise intelligence dashboards  

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 🌦️ Weather Intelligence
- Hyperlocal weather forecasting  
- AI-enhanced prediction layer over meteorological data  

### 🌾 Crop Monitoring
- NDVI-based crop health estimation  
- Risk alerts for farmers  

### 🌊 Disaster Prediction
- Early warning system for floods & droughts  
- Heatwave anomaly detection  

### 🛰️ Satellite Integration
- Earth observation data processing  
- State-level climate visualization  

### 📊 Interactive Dashboard
- India map-based visualization  
- State-wise climate analytics  
- Real-time insights panel
